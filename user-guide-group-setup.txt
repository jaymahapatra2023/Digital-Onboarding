Aside from licensing and verifying Compensable Code, users can take following actions
during this step:

1. If there are multiple brokers, the user has the option to split the commissions by
   entering the Commission Split percentages in the appropriate field under the
   Commission Split column. If there’s a single broker, the commission amount would be
   100%. For the last row Total Distribution, users can click on Distribute Equally to
   distribute 100% equally among the assigned writing producers.
Note:
Ø Total amount of the split must equal 100% and the user can view the total distribution
   amount at the bottom of the sheet after filling out the columns (only if commission split
   is entered).
2.   Click on the Delete icon under the Action column to delete an entry.
3.   Click on the Next button to go to the next step.
4.   Click on the Cancel button to cancel the process.
5.   Click on the Save as Draft button to save the progress as a draft and come back later
     before submitting.
Note:
Ø Once commission has been assigned to each broker, Commission Agreement
   Acknowledgement forms for all assigned brokers will be generated. If the writing
   producers were not verified in systems or commission was not assigned, user
   (Broker/GA/TPA) won’t be able to proceed with Commission Agreement
   Acknowledgement.
Ø User will be notified of any errors in confirming their licensing and
   appointment/compensable code so that they can continue with Group Setup process.

13 Error Details

        unknown system failure. Resolution of these errors generally requires technical
        analysis. You may contact your System Administrator for further assistance on these
